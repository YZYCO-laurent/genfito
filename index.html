Why willpower keeps losing.

Here's what most diets never tell you. When you eat fast-digesting carbs or sugar, your blood sugar can spike. Your body answers that spike with a rush of insulin — and that overcorrection sends you crashing.

That crash is what you feel: the sudden hunger, the sugar craving, the 3pm slump, the “why am I starving, I just ate?”

Spike, crash, craving, snack, spike again. Round and round. The longer you ride it, the harder weight feels to control — even when you're doing everything right.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is Genfito Different from Fat Burners and Weight Loss Pills? +
Genfito is not a fat burner or stimulant-based weight loss pill. It works by supporting your body's natural glucose response after meals — helping to reduce post-meal spikes that drive cravings, energy crashes, and inconsistent eating habits. No jitters, no crash, no stimulants.
How is Genfito Different from Other Weight Loss Supplements? +
Most supplements target a single mechanism. Genfito combines five studied actives — including the more-absorbable DHB form of berberine, White Mulberry Leaf Extract, Gymnema Sylvestre, Cinnamon Extract, and Chromium — each selected to support a different aspect of post-meal glucose management.
How Fast Can I Expect to See Noticeable Results? +
Many users report noticing steadier energy and reduced afternoon crashes within the first few days. Meaningful reductions in cravings and improved consistency typically emerge over the first few weeks. The full benefit builds over 2–3 months of consistent daily use.
Can Genfito Cause Bloating? +
Genfito is formulated to be gentle on digestion. A small number of users may experience mild digestive adjustment in the first few days, which typically resolves. Taking the capsules with food (as directed) helps minimize any discomfort.
Does Genfito Contain Allergens? +
Genfito is free from gluten, dairy, soy, and common allergens. It is vegan and gelatin-free. Please review the full ingredient list on the label if you have specific sensitivities.
Is Genfito Safe for Pregnant or Breastfeeding Women? +
We recommend consulting your healthcare provider before taking any supplement during pregnancy or while breastfeeding.
How Does Genfito Interact with Other Medications? +
If you are taking prescription medications — particularly diabetes medications or blood thinners — please consult your doctor before starting Genfito. The active ingredients can influence blood glucose levels and may interact with certain medications.
