Why willpower
keeps losing.

Here's what most diets never tell you. When you eat fast-digesting carbs or sugar, your blood sugar can spike. Your body answers that spike with a rush of insulin — and that overcorrection sends you crashing. That crash is what you feel: the sudden hunger, the sugar craving, the 3pm slump, the “why am I starving, I just ate?”

Spike, crash, craving, snack, spike again. Round and round. The longer you ride it, the harder weight feels to control — even when you're doing everything right.
