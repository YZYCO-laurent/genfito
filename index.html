No. Genfito is a natural supplement, not a prescription drug. It does not mimic or contain GLP-1 agonists. It supports your body's own glucose response naturally, without injections, prescriptions, or the side effects associated with GLP-1 drugs.

Genfito is generally well-tolerated. Some people may experience mild digestive adjustment during the first few days. Taking it with food and water helps. It contains no stimulants, artificial fillers, or common allergens.

Genfito is designed for healthy adults who want to support their glucose metabolism. If you have been diagnosed with diabetes or prediabetes and are on medication, please consult your doctor before use.
Does it contain allergens? Is it vegan? +
Genfito is free from gluten, dairy, soy, and common allergens. It is vegan and gelatin-free. Please review the full ingredient list on the label if you have specific sensitivities.
Is it safe if I'm pregnant or breastfeeding? +
We recommend consulting your healthcare provider before taking any supplement during pregnancy or while breastfeeding.
Can I take it with my medications? +
If you are taking prescription medications — particularly diabetes medications or blood thinners — please consult your doctor before starting Genfito. The active ingredients can influence blood glucose levels and may interact with certain medications.
